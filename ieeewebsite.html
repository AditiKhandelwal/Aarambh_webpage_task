WHAT IS AARAMBH?


Aarambh is an outreach program conducted by IEEE MANIT Students. Branch with an intrinsic aim to promote science not as a subject but as a part of day to day life and career interest to the

underprivileged sections of society. Needless to justify, children need to realize the importance of scientific point of view in their school days only, so that as they grow up, they can have a rational

perspective and live a healthy and prosperous lives.Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers endeavours to promote awareness among students on the opportunities that exist in the

endless world of science.An outreach program to promote elementary science education among school students. IEEE members make innovative projects to teach basic science in

everyday life concepts with waste materials and scratch which could be easily available in any household. Easy to make nature and low cost make the projects

enthralling and involving. The topics are selected from their syllabus to make the session worthy for students.
